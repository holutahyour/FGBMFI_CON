Chairman Speech

Chairman's Speech

Welcome to 2022 Abuja National Convention of the Full Gospel Business Men’s Fellowship International scheduled for 10th to 12th November,2022 in Abuja-Federal Capital Territory, Nigeria. We believe God that this will be a turning point in your individual lives, families, businesses, professions and most importantly, in your personal walk with our God. This Convention is planned with you in mind. We have 23 venues in Nigeria including the Convention Center in Abuja. Wherever you are in Nigeria there are about two to three convention venues within 200 kilometers radius of your national positioning. Those of you outside the shores of Nigeria can be part of this convention too by joining online (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, etc.) or better still attend physically. We genuinely encourage you to be expectant, “for the expectations of the Righteous shall not be dashed” The theme for this Convention is “Thou art my battle axe” It has please our God to express His will and Purposes on earth in our day and time through you and me. All He is looking for are those who will say “Here am I send me.” Are you willing to be one of those He will use to express Himself? Yes, the set time is here and now is. This will be the beginning of three days of tremendous, concentrated outpouring of naked and raw power of our God to galvanize hearts and destinies of men and women into a new realm of grace and power. Men and women who will corporately and individually disperse into all the corners of Nigeria, Africa, and the world loaded with anointing to showcase the supremacy of the Kingdom of God for this end-time. We know and believe that we all shall be drenched with dew from heaven and the inhabitants of this earth shall see our God at work in our day. Just prayerfully plan, take a step further by registering now, book an accommodation close to the nearest venue to you and attend this Convention. Invite your friends, family, neighbours and colleagues and even your enemies to this divine visitation. A new generation of God’s battle axes and weapons of warfare are emerging- Be a part of it. More graces to you all.

Surv. Victor Emmanuel
